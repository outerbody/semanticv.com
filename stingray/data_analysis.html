Information has become a firehose. Companies need to be able to navigate their own documents efficiently. We can help.

Stingray Data Analysis and Intelligence

It is estimated that 5 exabytes (1 billion gig) of unique information was generated in 2002 ( Berkeley ). It’s more today. Do you have to make sense of it all for your company? Stingray is a semantic technology that can find meaning in it all. Stingray doesn’t need to know what the documents are about, it will create a navigable concept map to help you find what you need. And make sense of it all.

Stingray can…

Learn meanings for words based on how they are used.

  • Definitions and meaning are relative to the vertical nature of the data set. Stingray can ascertain meanings specific to your document set.

Explore information using concepts

  • Wouldn't it be nice if all of your documents were categorized? Wouldn't it be nice if it was done for you automatically, regardless of how many documents you had? Stingray can also guide you through relevant paragraphs, sentences, or words, drawing your attention to regions where targeted topics are discussed.

Discover themes in a body of text

  • Stingray can summarize all of the content for you in a document set, and allow you to easily navigate your information.
